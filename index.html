Welcome to Chronologic!

This is a round-way ticket to the past. In this interactive timeline you will be able to explore events in the History of Civilization. The essential events were curated using Cesare Emiliani’s Holocene calendar for you to entertain yourself while exploring it visually.

What is the Holocene calendar?

The Holocene calendar or The Human Era calendar it’s a 1993 proposal by Cesare Emiliani to solve a number of problems with the current Anno Domini era.

Chronologic uses the Human Era calendar because it places its first year near the beginning of the Holocene geological epoch & the Neolithic Revolution, when humankind started to abandon the hunting/gathering lifestyle in exchange for agriculture and fixed settlements.

When converting Gregorian AD years to the Holocene Calendar it can be achieved by adding 10,000 years to the AD year, therefore the current year 2018 would convert to 12,018.
